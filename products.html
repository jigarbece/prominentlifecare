Product 01 · System

PromQ Prime

A multipurpose Type I water maker that delivers ultrapure water simultaneously, with very low TOC content — meeting the requirements of liquid chromatography, all spectroscopy methods, microbiological and molecular biology applications.

Compact · Smart · Reliable

Single-touch dispensing.
Continuous purity.

Dispensing of ultrapure water is configured on the front panel, while pre-water is dispensed from the integrated storage tank. For feed water with higher TDS, a customised pre-treatment module can be designed prior to PromQ Prime — making the system adaptable to virtually any laboratory feed source.

Built to ISO 9001:2015 and CE compliance standards, PromQ Prime combines reverse osmosis, dual-wavelength UV, ion-exchange resin and ultrafiltration in one compact, portable cabinet.

ISO 9001:2015 CE Compliant Dual UV 185/254 nm 7-Stage Filtration

Prime · Flow Schematic

Inside the PromQ Prime.

A precision-tuned, multi-stage process that transforms raw feed water into Type I ultrapure water — quietly, consistently, and reliably.

01
Pre-Filtration

Sediment & particulate removal at the inlet stage.

02
EPS Cell

Energy-pulse cell stage to enhance downstream filtration.

03
Low-Pressure Switch

Triggers dry-run protection when feed pressure drops.

04
PromQ Clear Filter

Polishes water before booster pressurisation.

05
Booster Pump

Anodised aluminium pump at 1.8 LPM nominal flow.

06
RO Membrane

Ionic rejection > 99% via reverse-osmosis stage.

07
Super Cartridge

Mixed-bed resin polishing — 5× 2.5″ × 10″ UPC.

08
Storage Tank

30 / 60 L reservoir with UV-254nm sanitisation.

09
UV-18 Lamp

Dual-wavelength 185 / 254 nm reduces TOC.

10
Resistivity Sensor

Cell K = 0.01 with SS 316L electrodes.

11
UF Filter

Ultra-filtration to remove pyrogens & endotoxins.

12
0.22 µm Capsule

Hydrophilic Nylon-66 final point-of-use filter.

Feed water is first de-mineralised by membrane filtration / ion exchange to ~ 0.5 µS/cm, then polished through a high-performance Mixed Bed to reach a final resistivity of 18.2 MΩ.

Prime · Pre-Treatment

Adaptable to any feed water.

For sites with higher TDS or challenging raw water, a customised pre-treatment module — sediment filter, booster, super-orb & sorb, auto cut-off — feeds clean water into PromQ Prime for consistent Type I output.

B
Booster Pump

C

Multi-media polishing for organic & chlorine load.

D
PromQ Sorb

Activated carbon & ion-exchange media stage.

Product 02 · Bottled

PromQ Water

Pre-bottled HPLC & Spectroscopy grade water — ultrapure Type I water, sealed in inert glass and ready to use straight from the bottle. Lot traceable, batch certified and consistent across every shipment.

Ready-to-use · Certified · Consistent

Open the bottle.
Use it instantly.

For laboratories without a permanent purification system — or as a reliable backup beside your PromQ Prime — PromQ Water delivers HPLC & spectroscopy grade water in inert glass packaging, with the same purity guarantees as our purification systems.

Every bottle is filtered through a 0.22 µm membrane, sealed under sanitised conditions and shipped with a Certificate of Analysis quoting the production batch — perfect for regulated environments where traceability is non-negotiable.
