Product 01 · System

PromQ Prime

A multipurpose Type I water maker that delivers ultrapure water simultaneously, with very low TOC content — meeting the requirements of liquid chromatography, all spectroscopy methods, microbiological and molecular biology applications.

Compact · Smart · Reliable

Single-touch dispensing.
Continuous purity.

Dispensing of ultrapure water is configured on the front panel, while pre-water is dispensed from the integrated storage tank. For feed water with higher TDS, a customised pre-treatment module can be designed prior to PromQ Prime — making the system adaptable to virtually any laboratory feed source.

Built to ISO 9001:2015 and CE compliance standards, PromQ Prime combines reverse osmosis, dual-wavelength UV, ion-exchange resin and ultrafiltration in one compact, portable cabinet.

ISO 9001:2015 CE Compliant Dual UV 185/254 nm 7-Stage Filtration

Product 02 · Bottled

PromQ Water

Pre-bottled HPLC & Spectroscopy grade water — ultrapure Type I water, sealed in inert glass and ready to use straight from the bottle. Lot traceable, batch certified and consistent across every shipment.

Ready-to-use · Certified · Consistent

Open the bottle.
Use it instantly.

For laboratories without a permanent purification system — or as a reliable backup beside your PromQ Prime — PromQ Water delivers HPLC & spectroscopy grade water in inert glass packaging, with the same purity guarantees as our purification systems.

Every bottle is filtered through a 0.22 µm membrane, sealed under sanitised conditions and shipped with a Certificate of Analysis quoting the production batch — perfect for regulated environments where traceability is non-negotiable.
